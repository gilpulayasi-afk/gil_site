Bilateral Project

The Centre of Excellence in Information Technology (CEIT) is an initiative of the Papua New Guinean and Indian Governments to bridge the gap between academia and industry and to create a pool of knowledgeable IT workers, generate employment opportunities by producing world-class IT professionals.
