Certificate Program in Database Management

The objective of this program is to provide students with expertise in Database Technologies and Administration.
Duration : 16 - 18 Weeks (320 Hours)     
Eligibility : Minimum of Grade 12 Certificate    
Program Prerequisite : Basic knowledge of computers.

SL No. Courses Hours
1 Fundamentals of Computer and OS Concepts 20
2 C Programming 40
3 Software Development Lifecycle 10
4 Database Technologies 80
5 Database Administration 70
6 Management Development Program 40
7 Capstone Project 60
