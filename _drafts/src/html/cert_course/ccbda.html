Certificate Program in Big Data Analytics

The objective of this Program in Big Data Analytics is to provide students with expertise in Big Data Analytic domain. Analyze the big data using Statistics with R, Data Visualization - Analysis and Reporting, Business Analytics.
Duration : 16 - 18 Weeks (320 Hours)     
Eligibility : Minimum of Grade 12 Certificate    
Prerequisite : Fair knowledge of Computing Fundamentals and Fundamentals of Programming, Database Technology and Java Programming.

SL No. Courses Hours
1 Big Data Fundamentals 30
2 Statistical Analysis with R 90
3 Data Visualization - Analysis and Reporting 40
4 Business Analytics 60
5 Management Development Program 40
6 Capstone Project 60
