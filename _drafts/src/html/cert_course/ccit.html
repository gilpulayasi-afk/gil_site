Certificate Program in Information Technology

The objective of this program is to provide students with expertise in IT for Management, Office Automation tools, and Database and Networking concepts.
Duration : 12 - 14 Weeks (250 Hours)     
Eligibility : Minimum of Grade 12 Certificate    
Program Prerequisite : Fair knowledge of Basic Computer Fundermentals
