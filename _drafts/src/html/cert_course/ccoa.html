Certificate Program in Office Automation

The objective of this program is to enable students to understand the Operating System and Office Automation tools.
Duration : 7 - 8 Weeks (150 Hours)     
Eligibility : Minimum of Grade 12 Certificate    
Program Prerequisite : Sound knowledge of Basic Computer Fundamentals.

SL No. Courses Hours
1 Fundamentals of Computer and OS Concepts 20
2 Client Software Systems 20
3 Office Automation Tools 60
