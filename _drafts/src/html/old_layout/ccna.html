Certificate Course in Network Administration

This course is aimed to provide skills on networking and its maintenance and will help the students to make a career in Network management.
Duration : 320 Hrs      Course Fee : K----
Eligibility : Minimum of Grade 12 Certificate     NSQF Level : 7
Course Prerequisite : Candidate should have basic knowledge of computer, Operating system and networking fundamentals with logical approach.

SL No. Modules Hours
1 Fundamentals of Networking 40
2 Network Administration 100
3 Network Defense and Countermeasures 80
4 Management Development Program 60
5 Project 40

Outcome : These candidates will be trained in networking, Network Administration and Network Defense & CounterMeasures skills. Network administrators can work in a variety of industries, ranging from telecommunications to security exchanges.
After the completion of the course, students can work as System Administrator / Network Administrator / Network Support and associated service sectors.
