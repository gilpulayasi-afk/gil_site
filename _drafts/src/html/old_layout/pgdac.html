PG-Diploma in Advanced Computing

The course is targeted towards engineers and IT professionals who wish to venture into the domain of advanced computing. This course covers on domain of MEAN Stack, Java, MS NET development

Duration : 900 Hours Course Fee : K----
Eligibility : Graduate in Engineering in IT / Computer Science / Electronics / Telecommunications / Electrical / Instrumentation or B.Sc in Mathematics / Statistics / Computer Science / Physics. NSQF Level : 8


Course Prerequisite : Sound knowledge of Computing Fundamentals and Fundamentals of Programming.
