Certificate Course in Multimedia Website Designing

The objective of this course is to provide knowledge to the participants who complete this course and to make a career in web designing & Animation activities.
Duration : 320 Hrs      Course Fee : K----
Eligibility : Minimum of Grade 12 Certificate     NSQF Level : 7
Course Prerequisite : Candidate should have basic knowledge of computer, Operating system and networking fundamentals with logical approach.

1 Introduction to Computers 20

5 Management Development Program 60
6 Project 40

Outcome : These candidates will be trained in Flash Animation, Scripting and UI Designing skills.Website Designer can work in a variety of industries, ranging from telecommunications to security exchanges After the completion of the course, students can work as Website Designer and associated service sectors.
